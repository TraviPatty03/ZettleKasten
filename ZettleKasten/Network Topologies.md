### **Definition**

A **Network Topology** refers to the arrangement of various elements (links, nodes) in a computer network.

### **Types of Topologies**

1. **Bus Topology**
2. **Star Topology**
3. **Ring Topology**
4. **Mesh Topology**
5. **Tree Topology**
6. **Hybrid Topology**
