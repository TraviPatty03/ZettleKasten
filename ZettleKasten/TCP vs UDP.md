#### **Definition**

Comparison between **TCP (Transmission Control Protocol)** and **UDP (User Datagram Protocol)**.

#### **TCP**

- Connection-oriented.
- Reliable data transfer.
- Error checking and flow control.

#### **UDP**

- Connectionless.
- Faster but less reliable.
- Minimal error checking.
