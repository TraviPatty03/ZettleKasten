### Methods

- **VPNs (Virtual Private Networks)**: Encrypts your connection and hides your IP.
- **Proxy Servers**: Acts as an intermediary between your device and the internet.
- **Tor Browser**: Routes your traffic through multiple servers for anonymity.

### Best Practices

- **Regularly Update Software**: Protects against vulnerabilities.
- **Use Firewalls**: Blocks unwanted incoming traffic.
- **Be Cautious Online**: Avoid clicking on suspicious links.
